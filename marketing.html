No More Snooze

Win Your Morning.

The Alarm Clock You Can't Ignore.

Tired of hitting the snooze button? NoMoreSnooze turns waking up into a game. To turn off the alarm, you must complete a mission that activates your brain:

  • Solve math problems
  • Complete color-matching puzzles
  • Get out of bed and take steps
  • Scan a specific barcode

Build streaks, earn XP, and collect lives. But be warned: if you use the Emergency Snooze, it'll cost you a life!
